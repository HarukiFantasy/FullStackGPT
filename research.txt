XZ backdoor research summary: The XZ backdoor was discovered in March 2024 in XZ Utils, a popular open-source data compression utility used in many Linux distributions. It was found in versions 5.6.0 and 5.6.1, allowing remote access and execution of code with root privileges. The backdoor was reported on March 28, 2024, and assigned CVE-2024-3094. This incident highlights significant security risks associated with supply chain attacks in open-source software.
